ABOUT

I am a mathematician, trained in differential topology and gauge theory — six years of PhD studies at Imperial College London under Sir Simon Donaldson, building on an earlier degree in physics (Diplom, University of Turin, 110/110).

I have taught at university level in Berlin for over twenty years — at HTW Berlin, Berliner Hochschule für Technik, and TU Berlin — and at the Nesin Mathematics Village in Turkey since 2019. Alongside this, I have tutored privately for thirty years: roughly a thousand students, from school exams to graduate coursework, with eighty reviews on Superprof, all five stars.

Most students come to me stuck — behind in a course, facing an exam in a few weeks, or simply not getting what they need from a lecture of two hundred people. I work one-to-one, at your pace, until it actually makes sense.

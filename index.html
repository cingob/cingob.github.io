ABOUT

I studied physics in Turin and pure mathematics at Imperial College London, where I began a doctorate in differential topology and gauge theory under Sir Simon Donaldson. I have spent the years since teaching — at universities in Berlin, at the Nesin Mathematics Village in Turkey, and privately, to students preparing for some of the most demanding examinations in Europe.

I work with students the way I was taught: carefully, rigorously, and with patience for the moment when something difficult finally becomes clear.
